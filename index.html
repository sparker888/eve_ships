Amarr Weapon Types

Long Range: Beam

Longest: Aurora (T2)

Long: Microwave

Medium: I.N. Multifrequency (T2)

Shortest: Gleam (T2)

Short Range: Pulse

Medium: Scorch (T2)

Short: I.N. Multifrequency (T2)

Use Conflag for slower close targets.

➵ Highest damage against weak natural resists of shields (Caldari & Minmatar)
➵ Lowest damage against strong natural resists of armor (Amarr & gallente)
➵ Highest CPU requirement of all weapons
➵ Heat Sink modules increase lasers' damage and rate of fire.
➵ Crystals: Long-range lose tracking/DPS. Short range gain tracking/DPS

Caldari Weapon Types

Missile Types

Rockets: Base 4.5km (13km)

Light Missiles: Base 19km (36km)

Heavy Assault Missiles: Base 7.5km (20km)

Heavy Missiles: Base 21km (40km)

Torpedoes: Base 9km (50km)

Cruise Missiles: Base 65km (200km)

Bombs: 30km AOE damage

Missile Damage Types:

Electromagnetic (EM): Mjolnir

Thermal: Inferno

Kinetic: Scourge

Thermal: Nova

For Light, Heavy, Cruise Launchers:

Precision: for small/fast targets (Tech II)

Fury: for large/slow targets (Tech II)

For Rocket, Heavy Assault, Torpedo Launchers:

Javelin: longer range/lower DPS (Tech II)

Rage: for shorter range/higher DPS (Tech II)

NOTE: Rage rockets are typically too slow for frigates so use faction ammo for small, fast ships.

➵ Missile range is determined by velocity and flight time (no falloff).
➵ Missile damage is determined by velocity and sig radius of the target, and explosion radius, explosion velocity and damage reduction factor.
➵ Missiles fire more slowly than turrets and have a travel time, but higher volley.
➵ Launchers have a reload time. You can stop firing and reload at any time.
➵ Faction ammo from least damage bonus to highest: Guristas, Caldari Navy, Dread Guristas.

➵ Against brawlers will try to kite out to 9km with overheated scram.
➵ Against kiters will try to slingshot and attempt to stay under guns, although difficult.
➵ Assuming autocannon fit, get to 0km and go all in OR keep them at range out past 9km.
➵ Typically an Kinteic/Explosive hole.
